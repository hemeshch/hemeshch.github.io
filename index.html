About Hemesh

Hemesh Chadalavada is a 17-year-old student innovator, entrepreneur and speaker.

At the age of 13, he received the Bal Puraskar, India’s highest civilian honour for students, for his creation the 'Alpha Monitor'. In 2022, Hemesh won the Samsung Solve for Tomorrow Contest and received a grant of $40,000.

In 2023, he was also awarded The Times of India's Unstoppable21.
