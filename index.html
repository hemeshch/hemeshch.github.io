My Innovations

  • Smart Wristband To Monitor Old and Disabled

    This Project is made to help Old and Disabled People and especially people suffering from Alzheimer's. It is a Wristband worn by the patient. It alerts the family member or caregiver if the patient gets out of his bed. An App displays the Pulse, Body Temperature and the Body Movements of the Patient. It can also detect if a person fell on the ground.

  • Robot To Help Old & Disabled

    This Robot helps Old and Disabled people do their Daily Tasks. It has a smart pillbox which reminds the person taking medicines at the right time. It also has smart fans and lights which turns on automatically when its Hot and Dark Respectively. It also has Amazon Echo Functionality, Fire alarm, smoke alarm, digital stethoscope and, a robot arm.

  • Smart Navigating Stick For the Blind

    This is a stick which helps blind people in navigation. With voice commands a person can navigate from one place to another. There are also motors at the bottom of the stick which moves in the direction the stick navigates. An Ultra-Sonic sensor helps in preventing a collision from any object.

  • Smart Seatbelt and Accident Notifier

    Car Accidents occur daily. So wearing a seatbelt can save their life. So I created a device known as the Smart Seatbelt. So if the person doesn't wear the Seatbelt the car WON'T START. There is also a sensor on the Seat Belt which monitors the Pulse Rate of the person. If an accident occurs or if the Pulse Rate of the person is low, the device detects it and calls the Ambulance.

  • Smart First - Aid Kit and Medicine Vending Machine

    I created this device to provide basic medication and health checkups to all. This device has a medicine vending machine which provides medicines according to the person's conditions.
