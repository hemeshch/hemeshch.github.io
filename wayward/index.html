Our story

Hemesh's grandmother was one of many, who was suffering from Alzheimer's disease.

She had severe cases of wandering, where she would walk away without the caregiver noticing, resulting in a number of accidents and injuries inside the home.

In some cases, she even wandered outside the home and got lost.
